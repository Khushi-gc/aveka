Overview —

In 2012 we joined Google on a mission to push the boundaries of innovation. During the course of our partnership we’ve developed new products, reimagined their most iconic brands, optimized their communications with the world, and influenced internal operations. We’ve iterated platforms such as YouTube and Search, plus designed new revenue-driving products while co-creating future visions for entire domains. We reimagined the Google Cloud Building, blending the physical and digital to create an ambitious, sustainable space and lead the Android rebrand, creating a global design system that’s architected with inclusivity at its core.
